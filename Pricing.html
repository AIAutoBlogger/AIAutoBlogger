Find the Perfect Plan for Your Store's Needs

Choosing the right plan is about identifying where you are in your business journey and what bottlenecks are holding you back. AI Auto Blogger is designed to be your automated content partner, scaling with you as you grow. Below, we break down who each plan is built for and the problems it solves.

The Basic Plan: For the Busy Founder and New Store Owner

Are you a solopreneur or a small team juggling everything from product sourcing and packing orders to customer service? You know that blogging is crucial for SEO, but finding the time to write is impossible. The Basic plan is your perfect entry point. It's designed for merchants whose primary bottleneck is time. Instead of letting your blog collect dust, you can establish a consistent content foundation effortlessly. This plan gets you on the board with SEO, building your online presence and attracting early organic traffic while you focus on the hundreds of other tasks required to run your business.

The SEO Growth Plan: For the Ambitious Store Ready to Scale

Perhaps your store is established, sales are steady, but you've hit a plateau in your organic traffic. You see competitors ranking for valuable keywords, and you know you need to produce more content to compete, but hiring a dedicated writer or agency is outside the budget. The SEO Growth plan is built for this exact scenario. It solves the scalability bottleneck. With 30 posts per month, you can move from just having a blog to executing a genuine content strategy. This plan empowers you to target a wider range of keywords, build topical authority in your niche, and significantly accelerate your traffic growth, giving you the competitive edge you need to pull ahead.

The Pro Plan: For the Market Leader and Power User

For high-volume stores and marketing teams in competitive niches, the goal isn't just to compete, it's to dominate. Your bottleneck is volume and market saturation. Manually creating enough content to become the definitive authority in your space would require a massive team and budget. The Pro plan is your solution. It's an enterprise-level content engine designed for maximum impact. With 90 posts a month, you can blanket your niche, answer every potential customer question through content, and capture a dominant share of search traffic. This plan is for businesses that see content as a core pillar of their marketing and want to achieve a scale that leaves competitors struggling to keep up.
