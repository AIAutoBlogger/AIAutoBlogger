AI Auto Blogger Shopify App Tutorial

Learn how to use AI Auto Blogger directly in your Shopify admin to create professional blogs from your ideas instantly.

1. Install AI Auto Blogger

Find AI Auto Blogger in the Shopify App Store and click “Add app.” Follow the quick prompts to connect it to your store.

2. Navigate to Blog Generator

From your Shopify admin, click the AI Auto Blogger icon to launch the app and start creating content.

3. Enter Your Blog Ideas

Add up to five blog ideas at once, each with a title and a short subject outline. Optionally, select products to feature so they’re automatically linked in your post.

4. Generate and Review

Click "Submit and Publish" to create full-length, SEO-ready blogs with optimized meta titles and descriptions. Review and edit as you wish. The content goes live on your Shopify store automatically.

5. Publish Your Blogs

Review your published posts and continue to the submit more blog ideas through the app to grow your store organically with the all-in-one content engine.
